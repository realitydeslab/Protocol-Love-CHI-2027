## Recommended Strategy

1. **Primary submission: CHI 2027** (~Sept 2026 deadline). This is the highest-impact venue and the paper's framing is squarely HCI.

2. **If CHI doesn't accept:** Revise for **CSCW** (rolling, submit immediately after CHI notification ~Jan 2027) or **DIS 2027** (~Jan 2027 deadline). CSCW is the natural second choice given the community governance and prosocial behavior literature.

3. **Parallel workshop:** Consider proposing a **CHI 2027 workshop** on "Constitutive Design for Prosocial Systems" to build community regardless of paper acceptance.

4. **Game-specific version:** A shorter, more practice-oriented version could target **CHI PLAY 2027** or **FDG 2027** if the theoretical framing needs to be lighter for a games audience.
